Customize Drink - Forms

This element is used for customizing the attributes of the drinks. It comes in the form of two components, the small screen drop down and the medium/large screen icons. In order to use them the icons must be named ICONNAME-active.png and ICONNAME.png. The components must be have an id of SECTION-NAME-big and SECTION-NAME for big and small respectively. A line needs to be added to the main.js, initCustomize("#SECTION-NAME"); in order to work. The JS will do the rest.
NOTE: Remember to set a default active icon.
